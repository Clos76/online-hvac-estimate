‚ùì Questions People Ask at This Stage

"How much can I really save vs. California?"

Typically 40-60% on construction costs. A $400K build in Baja would be $800K-$1.2M+ in coastal California for similar quality and size.

"Is it safe to buy property in Mexico?"

Yes, with the fideicomiso system. It's been in place since 1973 and provides strong legal protections. See Chapter 4 in the guide.

"Do I need to speak Spanish?"

Helpful but not required. We're a bilingual team and handle all contractor/official communications. Many of our clients speak minimal Spanish.

"How often do I need to be on-site?"

Depends on your preference. Some clients visit monthly, others rely on our weekly photo/video updates and visit just 3-4 times during the build.
